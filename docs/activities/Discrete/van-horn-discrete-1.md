# Discrete Structures 1 (Van Horn)

These activities by Jim Van Horn are available on Google Drive:
[Discrete Structures 1](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1FRKj19oLYRmDnxSkIyu9e-d-Yerq8skZ).

## Contents

1. Group Roles and the Problem Solving Process
2. Sequences and Series
3. Propositions and Truth Tables
4. Predicates
5. Multiple Quantifiers and Negation
6. Implications
7. Converse, Inverse, Contrapositive
8. Mathematical Writing
9. Proofs About Numbers
10. Proofs by Mathematical Induction
11. Proofs by Contradiction and the Pigeonhole Principle
12. Number Systems
13. Introduction to Sets
14. More Operations on Sets
15. Power Sets and Partitions
16. Set Properties and Boolean Algebra
17. Logic Circuits
18. Functions
19. Composition Operation
20. Properties of Functions
21. Properties of Relations
