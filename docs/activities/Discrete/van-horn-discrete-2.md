# Discrete Structures 2 (Van Horn)

These activities by Jim Van Horn are available on Google Drive:
[Discrete Structures 2](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1HLGjOntqO42-MegeIMlWdtEd9DmIh-JJ).

1. Introduction to Combinatorics
2. Rules of Product and Sum
3. More Counting and Permutations
4. Combinations
5. Binomial Theorem
6. Binary Sequences
7. Generalized Rules for Counting
8. Recursive Counting
9. More Counting
10. Difference Tables
11. Other Types of Recurrence Relations
12. Probability
13. Sum and Product Rules for Probability
14. Terms and Notation (Graph Theory)
15. Introduction to Graph Theory
16. Introduction to Trees with Minimal Spanning Trees
17. Minimal Spanning Trees and Isomorphism
18. Graph Isomorphism
19. Planar Graphs
20. Matrices and Directed Graphs
21. Directed Graphs and Relations
22. Binary Trees Introduction
23. Sorting and Traversing
24. Hamiltonian Cycles
25. Approximate Solutions to TSP
26. Graphs in Puzzles and Games
